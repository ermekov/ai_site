FAQ

Questions business owners ask before trusting AI with customers.

Can the AI answer in Kazakh and Russian?

Yes. We can structure the knowledge base and scripts for English, Kazakh, and Russian, then tune vocabulary and tone for your brand.

Will customers know they are talking to AI?

That depends on your policy. We usually recommend a transparent assistant that is fast, useful, and easy to hand off to a manager.

Can it connect to our CRM or Google Sheets?

Yes. We can send leads to CRM, Google Sheets, Telegram, or a custom workflow with fields like source, service, budget, urgency, and contact details.

What happens when the AI cannot answer?

The assistant can collect context and transfer the conversation to a manager with a short summary, so the customer does not repeat everything.

How fast can we launch?

A focused Start build can launch quickly after content and access are ready. Larger multi-channel systems need more testing because reliability matters.
